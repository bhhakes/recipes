This is a recipe for an Apple-Avocado Salad

Ingredients:
- 24 oz Bag of Mixed Green Salad (or your favorite type of lettuce)
- One-half honeycrisp apple
- One-half red pepper
- One Avocado
- Two tablespoons of olive oil
- Two tablespoons of balsamic vinegar

Preparation:
- Put mixed green salad into large bowl
- Dice apple and place in bowl with salad
- Dice red pepper and place in bowl
- Dice avocado and place in bowl
- Pour olive oil over salad bowl
- Pour balsamic vinegar over salad bowl
- Mix ingredients together

Enjoy!
